BARFI :

  • Ingredients used:
  • khoa (condensed milk)
  • granulated sugar
  1. process to make barfi:
  2. Firstly, in a bowl take 2½ cup milk powder, ¾ cup sugar and 1 cup milk.
  3. add ¼ cup ghee and mix well on low flame
  4. keep stirring on low flame until the mixture thickens.
